About Us

Alive and Modern is a new company that combines the best elements of nature and modern design to create stunning visuals for any room or office. We are starting out with a few different desktop-friendly aquariums, fountains, and Living Walls. Each piece is designed to bring a natural, wild feel into any room regardless of light levels, space, and room layout...

Our Mission

At Alive and Modern we see a gap between people, nature, and technology: Our goal is to bridge that gap. Every person benefits from bringing live plants and moving water into their living space. We want to make it as easy (and awesome-looking) as we can to bring these elements into any room or office.

Our Products

Alive and Modern produces the highest quality, hand-constructed aquariums, living walls and fountains. Each structure is equipped to support plant life with our special LED bulb and advanced wicking material that supports the plant root systems.
