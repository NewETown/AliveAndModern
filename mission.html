Our mission

...By merging these two concepts...

...And providing you with the ultimate desktop decorations.

Aquariums and living walls are great. They're calming, they look good, and they increase productivity, air quality, and aesthetic wherever they're set up. There are, however, several major drawbacks to aquariums and living walls today.

First and foremost: They tend to be quite large, at least too large to fit in an average office or small room. This was the easiest obstacle to overcome. At Alive and Modern we chose very specific dimensions for each of our structures; we focus on using vertical space while providing a wide-enough base for stability.

Maintenance is always an issue with any kind of living structure. We make it as easy as possible to maintain your living structure. Our aquariums take advantage of biological and mechanical filtration the same way nature does. Water is purified by the plants and movement down any of the various waterfall structures. Beneficial bacteria is able to build in the substrate and special material used to encourage plant growth. The combination of these elements creates a clean, health, and low-maintenance aquarium for you to enjoy.
